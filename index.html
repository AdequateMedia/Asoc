About True Worshippers

Founded in 2020 by Apostle Solomon S. Adebowale and Reverend Mrs. Debby Adebowale, True Worshippers Evangelical Ministries International is a spiritual family dedicated to raising authentic worshipers who encounter God in spirit and truth.

We are called to equip believers for the work of ministry, build up the body of Christ, and carry the message of reconciliation to the ends of the earth. Through sound teaching, daily devotionals, and worship, we foster an atmosphere where lives are transformed and identity in Christ is established.

Vision & Missions

Our divine mandate and core callings as a ministry

Vision

To raise a global family of authentic worshipers whose lives reflect the nature of the Father and who impact their world with the Gospel. We envision believers walking in spiritual maturity, cultural influence, and kingdom purpose.

"That they may be called oaks of righteousness, the planting of the Lord, that he may be glorified." — Isaiah 61:3

Worship

Igniting authentic worship through praise, prayer, and the Word, drawing believers into deeper intimacy with God and creating an atmosphere for His presence. We equip worship leaders and intercessors to lead with passion and truth.

"God is spirit, and those who worship him must worship in spirit and truth." — John 4:24

Discipleship

Establishing every believer in their identity in Christ through systematic teaching, life-transforming relationships, and the daily devotional "Sequences of God." We build foundations for spiritual growth and leadership development.

"Go therefore and make disciples of all nations... teaching them to observe all that I have commanded you." — Matthew 28:19-20

Outreach & Missions

Demonstrating God's love through compassion projects, evangelism, church planting, and equipping the next generation of leaders locally and internationally. We extend the kingdom through mercy and justice.

"But you will receive power when the Holy Spirit has come upon you, and you will be my witnesses... to the end of the earth." — Acts 1:8

Who's Son Are You?

Apostle Segun D. A. & Reverend Mrs. Debby F. A. Solomon
Hymn: “I’m a Child of the King”
"Your behavior is the loudspeaker of your paternity. You cannot be fathered by God and fathered by the world; your actions will always announce who truly owns you." – Apostle Segun D. A. Solomon

John 8:39-47 (NKJV)

They answered and said to Him, “Abraham is our father.” Jesus said to them, “If you were Abraham’s children, you would do the works of Abraham. But now you seek to kill Me, a Man who has told you the truth which I heard from God. Abraham did not do this. You do the deeds of your father.”... He who is of God hears God’s words; therefore you do not hear, because you are not of God.

Devotional Message

In the natural realm, lineage determines inheritance, rights, and often, behavior. In the spiritual realm, lineage determines destiny. The Pharisees in our text today were confident in their biological and religious pedigree. They boasted, "Abraham is our father." But Jesus, the discerner of thoughts, looked past their flesh and into their spirits and posed a question that shook the foundation of their identity: "Who's son are you?" He essentially told them, Your actions don't match your claims.

Many believers today walk in a similar identity crisis. We carry the Bible, sing the hymns, and wear the label "Christian," but our character, our speech, and our secret lives often tell a different story. We claim God as our Father, yet we display the temper, the pride, the dishonesty, and the lust of the world. As Reverend Mrs. Debby F. A. Solomon often teaches, "You cannot draw water from a bitter well and claim it is sweet; the source is revealed by the taste."

Jesus drew a clear line of demarcation: If God were your Father, you would love Me and obey the truth. If the devil were your father, you would fulfill his lusts—which include lies, murder, and a rejection of truth.

This devotion calls you to a spiritual paternity test. It is not enough to say you are born again; your life must exhibit the family resemblance of the One who is Spirit. Are you loving? God is love. Are you truthful? God is truth. Are you peaceful? God is not the author of confusion. When the pressure is on, what comes out of you reveals who is actually inside you.

Today, decide to align your conduct with your confession. Let the world see that you are indeed a son or daughter of the Most High, not by your boasting, but by your bearing of His image.

Prophetic Prayer Points

Father, I thank You because I am not a bastard but a legitimate child of the Most High God, bought by the blood of Jesus.
Lord, expose and uproot every seed of the wicked one planted in my life that does not reflect Your image.
I break and renounce every inherited negative trait from my earthly lineage that contradicts my identity in Christ.
Father, give me the grace to love what You love and hate what You hate, aligning my heart perfectly with Yours.
I silence the voice of the enemy that tries to speak through my flesh, and I amplify the voice of the Holy Spirit within me.
Lord, let the fruit of the Spirit (love, joy, peace) become the undeniable evidence of my divine paternity.
I reject the spirit of deception and lying; I embrace the truth, for I am of the Father of Truth.
Every ungodly character that does not represent my Father in Heaven, I command it to wither away now, in Jesus' name.
Father, help me to hear Your voice clearly and to obey Your words without hesitation, proving that I am truly of God.
I declare that my life will draw others to You, because they will see Your family resemblance in me.

Word of Encouragement

Beloved, do not be discouraged by the reflection you saw in the mirror of God's word today. If you look and see areas where the world's image is stronger than God's, take heart! We are not called to clean ourselves up so we can come to the Father; we come to the Father so He can clean us up. Your identity is not based on your perfection, but on your position in Christ. You are a son! And because you are a son, the DNA of heaven is inside you. Act like it. Talk like it. Walk like it. The world is waiting for the manifestation of the sons of God. Arise and shine, for your light has come, and the glory of the Lord is risen upon you!

God bless you. Go and show the world whose son you are!

— Apostle D.S.A. Solomon
